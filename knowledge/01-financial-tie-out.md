# 财务勾稽核查清单（HKFRS / IFRS，港股招股章程 Accountants' Report 适用）

> 招股章程财务数字出错最集中的区域。任一条不成立即列为 🔴 高风险。
> 港股报告常以 HK$'000 / RMB'000 / US$'000 列示，注意四舍五入尾差与币种。
> 实务中部分关系会因受限资金、合并范围变动、会计政策变更等存在合理差异，
> 须在 Notes 中有说明；若无说明则报疑点。

## 一、Statement of Financial Position（财务状况表 / 资产负债表）
| 编号 | 勾稽关系 |
|------|----------|
| SFP-1 | Total non-current assets = Σ 各非流动资产 |
| SFP-2 | Total current assets = Σ 各流动资产 |
| SFP-3 | Total assets = Total non-current assets + Total current assets |
| SFP-4 | Total current liabilities = Σ 各流动负债 |
| SFP-5 | Total non-current liabilities = Σ 各非流动负债 |
| SFP-6 | Total liabilities = current + non-current liabilities |
| SFP-7 | Total equity = Share capital + Reserves（含 Retained earnings 等）± Treasury shares |
| SFP-8 | **Total assets = Total liabilities + Total equity**（最重要恒等式）|
| SFP-9 | Equity attributable to owners of the Company + Non-controlling interests = Total equity |
| SFP-10 | 本期期初数 = 上一期间期末数（跨期连续）|

## 二、Statement of Profit or Loss（损益表）
| 编号 | 勾稽关系 |
|------|----------|
| PL-1 | Gross profit = Revenue − Cost of sales |
| PL-2 | Operating profit = Gross profit + Other income/gains − Selling & distribution − Administrative − Other expenses |
| PL-3 | Profit before tax = Operating profit − Finance costs (+ Share of results of associates/JV) |
| PL-4 | Profit for the year = Profit before tax − Income tax expense |
| PL-5 | Profit for the year = 归属于 Owners of the Company + Non-controlling interests |
| PL-6 | Basic EPS ≈ 归母利润 ÷ Weighted average no. of shares（重算核对）|

## 三、Statement of Cash Flows（现金流量表）
| 编号 | 勾稽关系 |
|------|----------|
| CF-1 | Net cash from operating activities = 经营流入 − 流出 |
| CF-2 | Net cash from investing activities = 投资流入 − 流出 |
| CF-3 | Net cash from financing activities = 筹资流入 − 流出 |
| CF-4 | Net increase in cash = 经营 + 投资 + 筹资 + 汇率影响 |
| CF-5 | Cash & cash equivalents at end = at beginning + net increase |

## 四、跨表 / 跨附注勾稽（最易忽略，重点）
| 编号 | 关系 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| X-1 | P/L 的 Profit = 现金流量表补充资料起点的 Profit | 应相等 |
| X-2 | Retained earnings 期末−期初 = 本期归母利润 − 宣派股息 ± 调整 | 经 Statement of Changes in Equity 勾稽 |
| X-3 | 现金流量表期末现金 vs SFP 的 Cash and bank balances | 差异=受限存款等，须有 Note |
| X-4 | 补充资料 Depreciation & amortisation vs PPE/无形资产 Notes | 应一致 |
| X-5 | Statement of Changes in Equity 逐列加总 = SFP 权益各科目期末−期初 | 逐项核 |
| X-6 | Revenue（P/L）= 分部/分产品/分地区收入明细（Notes/MD&A）合计 | 应相等 |
| X-7 | Trade receivables / Inventories（SFP）= 账龄分析表合计 | 应相等 |
| X-8 | 合并 NCI / 少数股东损益 与子公司持股比例 | 口径一致 |

## 五、指标重算公式（第 2 步用）
- Gross profit margin = (Revenue − Cost of sales) ÷ Revenue
- Net profit margin = Profit for the year ÷ Revenue
- Current ratio = Current assets ÷ Current liabilities；Quick ratio =（流动资产−Inventories）÷ 流动负债
- Gearing ratio（按章程定义口径，常见 = Total debt ÷ Total equity 或 Net debt ÷ Equity，须与定义一致）
- Trade receivables turnover days = 平均应收 ÷ Revenue × 期间天数
- Inventory turnover days = 平均存货 ÷ Cost of sales × 期间天数
- YoY growth = (本期 − 上期) ÷ |上期|

> 重算与披露值偏差超四舍五入容差（一般 0.1 个百分点 / 1 个尾数单位）即报疑点。
